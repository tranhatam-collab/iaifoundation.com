Privacy

Privacy Policy

IAI Foundation collects only the information needed to operate the public site, respond to partner or builder inquiries, and prepare governed Intent OS pilots.

Information we handle

We may process contact details, inquiry content, organization names, technical logs, and basic analytics. Production identity, payment, device, and proof workflows require separate provider-specific notices before activation.

How information is used

Information is used to respond to inquiries, protect the site, route partner conversations, evaluate pilots, and maintain auditability for approved workflows.

Security and retention

We use reasonable safeguards for public-site operations. Sensitive credentials, private keys, tax data, payment data, and session material must not be submitted through public contact channels.

Contact

For privacy requests, contact hello@iaifoundation.com.
